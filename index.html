[ Passages ]

Where the fleet goes.

Set routes we know in every weather, or the seed of one you bring us. Distances are rhumb-line and honest; the sea will add to them. The forecast is a suggestion — the sea keeps its own counsel.

  • The Western Approaches

    Falmouth · Scillies · Falmouth

    Out past the Manacles to the Isles of Scilly, anchoring where the chart runs out of names. A classic first passage — open water, but never far from a lee.

    0 nm 6 nights · Kestrel

  • Hebridean Traverse

    Oban · Sound of Mull · Small Isles

    Sheltered water and big scenery — the Sound of Mull, then out to Muck, Eigg and Rùm. Tidal gates to time and seals to keep the dawn watch company.

    0 nm 5 nights · Saskia

  • The Channel Islands

    Dartmouth · Guernsey · Sark

    A short, tide-ruled hop to the last of the old Channel anchorages. Sark has no cars and Sark has no rush; the tidal streams see to the rest.

    0 nm 3 nights · Perdita

  • The Norwegian Leads

    Bergen · Sognefjord · the skerries north

    Inside the skerries where the water goes still and black, threading the leads north from Bergen. Long light, cold nights, and depths the lead line won't reach.

    0 nm 8 nights · Saskia

  • The Solent Classic

    Cowes · Beaulieu · Yarmouth

    A weekend of sheltered short-tacking and the finest harbours in the south — for a first taste of the fleet, or a last one before the boats come ashore.

    0 nm 2 nights · Wren
CREW

[ The crew ]

You are in good hands.

Every passage is skippered by someone who has made it before, often in worse weather than you'll ever see. They are quiet, unhurried, and very hard to alarm.

Iain Rasmussen

Master · Kestrel & Saskia

Forty years at sea, half of them delivering other people's yachts across the Atlantic. Navigates by GPS and checks it against the stars out of habit. Will not raise his voice, even when you'd understand it if he did.

RYA Yachtmaster Ocean · 84,000 nm logged

Marta Holm

Mate & navigator · Perdita

Grew up sailing the Norwegian leads and knows every rock in them by its first name. Keeps the paper chart up to date because she doesn't trust a screen to survive a knockdown. Bakes bread on passage; the galley is her watch.

RYA Yachtmaster Offshore · celestial nav

Edvard Solheim

Bosun & relief skipper · Wren

Shipwright by trade — he built half of Wren's interior with his own hands and can fix anything aboard with what's already aboard. Teaches the knots, the reef, and the reason a boat under sail is quieter than any room ashore.

Shipwright · Coastal Skipper · 22 seasons
